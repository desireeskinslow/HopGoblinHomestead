Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to bring my own container?
Nope! Bringing your own is great, but there are always jars and containers available at the table. No stress.
What kinds of products do you carry?
Liquid refills (soap, cleaners), mushroom and garden goods, small handmade items, brewing-inspired curiosities, herb blends, and practical low-waste swaps.
Are products available every week?
Most staples are consistent, but seasonal items rotate. Check the current list before you visit—it updates regularly.
How do refills work?
Bring or grab a container, fill what you need, and pay by weight or volume. Simple as that. Labels and instructions provided.
Where can I find you next?
Check the "Find Me" section below for upcoming market dates and locations. Updates posted weekly.

About the Homestead

Hop Goblin Homestead is a small, curious operation run from a real homestead. It started with too many mushrooms, a soap experiment, and a hop vine that wouldn't quit.

Everything here is made, grown, or sourced with intention—low-waste, practical, and a little bit clever. No grand claims, just useful goods from someone who likes making things and has a quirky sense of humor.

The goblin is mostly metaphorical. Mostly.
