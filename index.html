The story

About the Homestead

Practical magic from the workshop

Started with Too Many Mushrooms

Hop Goblin Homestead is a real working homestead that happens to make and grow things worth sharing.

It started with a soap experiment, too many mushrooms, and a hop vine that refuses to quit. Now it's a practical operation: we make useful things, grow interesting goods, experiment constantly, and share the results with neighbors who care about zero-waste, handmade, and actually functional.

Everything here is made, grown, or sourced with intention. No grand claims. Just small-batch goods from someone who likes making stuff, learning by doing, and occasionally discovering something worth a market table.

The goblin is mostly metaphorical. Mostly.
