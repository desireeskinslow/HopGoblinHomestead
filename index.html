Good to Know

Do I need to bring my own container?

No. Bringing your own is encouraged, but there will usually be jars or containers available.

What kinds of products do you carry?

Refill station basics, garden goods, mushrooms, herbs, jars, handmade items, and small-batch experiments.

Are products available every week?

Not always. Some items are regular staples, and others change with the season, the garden, and whatever experiment is currently behaving.

How do refills work?

Products are priced by volume. Bring a clean container, choose what you want, and pay for the amount filled.

Where can I find you next?

Check the Find Me section or follow social updates for current markets, pop-ups, and availability.

About the Homestead

Useful little goods from the workshop table

Hop Goblin Homestead is a small, curious operation run from a real homestead. It started with too many mushrooms, a soap experiment, and a hop vine that wouldn’t quit.

Everything here is made, grown, or sourced with intention: low-waste, practical, useful, and a little bit clever. No grand claims — just small-batch goods from someone who likes making things, solving problems, and sharing the results.

The goblin is mostly metaphorical. Mostly.
